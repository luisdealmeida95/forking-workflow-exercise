* 2 cups pre-cooked corn flour
* 2.5 cups water
* 1 tbsp salt

Steps
1. Mix ingredients in a bowl and kneed until dough is uniform and does not stick to the skin. Add more flour or water if necessary.
2. Form the dough into balls of around 7cm diameter
3. Roll/press the balls into a disk shape
4. Heat a skillet and lightly oil the surface
5. Place the disks on the skillet and fry until a dark spot forms in the center
6. Flip the disks and repeat
7. Heat the oven to 190C and place the disks inside
8. Check the arepas by tapping them lightly in the center. If they sound like a drum, remove from the oven.
9. Serve with prefered fillings.
